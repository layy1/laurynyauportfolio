About

Meet the designer

I am a designer and creative based in Vancouver. I am pursuing bachelor's degree at Simon Fraser University studying Interactive Arts and Technology. I have an interest in UI/UX, branding and visual design. I strive to make unique and intuitive experiences that resonate with users.

I believe that collaboration, creative storytelling and thorough research are the key ingredients needed to create successful, user-friendly applications.

Being a student I am constantly learning and seeking new opportunities to exercise my skills in visual design, user-research, testing and programming and grow my experiences as a designer to go above and beyond. I hope to pursue a career in user interface and user experience design and utilize my skills and passions in front-end design.

In my personal time, I enjoy expanding my literary knowledge by reading, baking for the people I care about and expressing my creativity through drawing or writing. Additionally, I find fulfillment in volunteering and actively seek out opportunities to contribute to meaningful organizations in my community.
