Case Competition: Marketing Strategy

Team Marketing Strategy

Project Overview

Participated in SFU Student Marketing Association (SMA) Brandstorm 2023 case competition. In a team of 3, we devised a one-year marketing plan for a client to attract their target audience and increase engagement. The final deliverables to showcase our ideas included developing a brand strategy, building on Frontline Real Estate’s brand identity and creating content mockups. My group achieved 2nd place in the competition with our proposed solution.

Proposed Marketing Strategy

We split our final marketing strategy into a 1-year long campaign that combined video campaigns, graphic social media posts, networking events and a central interface, which played a large role in our campaign. This central interface consisted of a geo-guesser game that highlighted locations Frontline Real Estate primarily worked in, with a cameo of their office building for prospective hires to see where they could potentially be working. We created a timeline of how content would be rolled out with the end goal of increasing Frontline’s social media follower to engagement ratio by 5% and attracting new brokers.
